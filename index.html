Ayrton Senna

The Greatest Natural Talent in Formula 1

Here's a short timeline of Ayrton Senna's life:

  • 1960 – Born in São Paulo, Brazil.
  • 1981 – Moves to England to pursue a racing career in open-wheel cars.
  • 1984 – Makes his Formula 1 debut with Toleman.
  • 1985 – Joins Lotus and earns his first Grand Prix victory.
  • 1988 – Wins his first World Championship with McLaren.
  • 1990–1991 – Wins two more World Championships.
  • 1994 – Tragically dies at the San Marino Grand Prix, changing F1 safety forever.

Ayrton Senna is remembered not only for his breathtaking skill behind the wheel but also for his devotion to charity, his humility, and the inspiration he continues to provide to generations of racers and fans.
